Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HostUp's remote Airbnb co-hosting services. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly.

We manage your entire Airbnb operation remotely through a combination of technology and local partnerships. Our team handles all guest communication via Airbnb's platform, coordinates with trusted local vendors for cleaning and maintenance, uses smart locks for keyless entry, and employs dynamic pricing tools to optimize your rates. You maintain ownership while we handle day-to-day operations from our command center.

We currently provide full-service Airbnb management in three major Indian cities: Jaipur, Delhi, and Mumbai. These cities were chosen for their high tourist demand, strong short-term rental markets, and our established vendor networks. We're planning to expand to Bangalore, Goa, and Udaipur by 2024.

We charge a 15-25% commission on confirmed bookings, with rates negotiated based on your property type, location, and requirements. This means:

  • No upfront setup fees
  • No hidden charges or monthly retainers
  • We only earn when you earn
  • All services included in this single fee
  • Flexible pricing based on your property's potential

Our success is directly tied to maximizing your revenue, ensuring we're always motivated to optimize your property's performance.

We work with various property types, but some perform exceptionally well:

  • Entire apartments/homes (1-4 bedrooms)
  • Serviced apartments in business districts
  • Heritage properties and unique spaces
  • Well-located properties near tourist attractions or business centers
  • Modern amenities (WiFi, AC, parking if possible)

Properties should be in legal compliance for short-term rentals and preferably furnished to guest-ready standards.

The timeline depends on your property's readiness:

  • Property assessment: 1-2 days
  • Professional photography: 3-5 days
  • Listing creation & optimization: 2-3 days
  • Smart lock installation (if needed): 1-2 days
  • Vendor network setup: 1-2 days

Total timeline: 7-14 days from signing to receiving your first booking. Ready-to-rent properties can be live within a week.

Our dedicated guest support team handles all guest interactions 24/7:

  • 24/7 hotline for guest emergencies
  • Emergency maintenance coordination
  • Security issues and incident management
  • Medical emergencies assistance and local support
  • Local emergency contacts in each city

You'll be notified of any significant issues, but we handle the immediate response and resolution to ensure guest safety and satisfaction.

Your earnings flow directly to you with complete transparency:

  • Direct payments: Airbnb pays you directly per their standard schedule
  • Our commission: Deducted monthly via bank transfer or invoice
  • Monthly reports: Detailed breakdown of all earnings and deductions
  • Real-time dashboard: Track your earnings and performance anytime

You maintain full control of your Airbnb account and banking details. We never hold your money - it flows directly from Airbnb to your account.
